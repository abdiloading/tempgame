Celestial King

"A journey through light and memory."

INTRODUCTION

The game is designed for players to explore, complete quests, and take on different challenges. These challenges are not just battles, but also include rhythm, trivia or puzzles. Each zodiac sign is an enemy and has its own unique challenge. The game progresses through each one. I built the game using Unreal Engine for the player to enjoy battling while completing their objective to collect a zodiac sign.

Creating Celestial King was a challenging but rewarding journey that pushed me creatively and technically. I explored the gameplay mechanics by merging 2D Characters, Rhythm, Turn - Based System, the quest system and the visual novel system into an entire game. Through research, experimentation, and inspiration from global games and cultural history, I crafted a narrative and interactive experience that reflects my ability to merge storytelling with technical design and game mechanics, pushing genre boundaries while connecting with a wide audience, mainly in America and Asia, and hope to light a spark in their hearts for Europe, Africa and South America.

Through character-driven arcs, this game invites players to challenge destiny, and redefine what power truly means.
